Branding Design for LLS Organization

Year: 2024
Medium: Adobe Photoshop, Canva
Dimension: 1526 x 2154 (Pixel)

I was inspired to redesign the logo for the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society (LLS), a nonprofit organization dedicated to the fight against cancer. This project allowed me to dive deeper into typography, graphic design elements, and overall design principles. Above all, I focused on enjoying the process of recreating the organization's logo.

In this project, I honed my editing skills in Canva and Photoshop while designing mockups for a shirt and hoodie. I incorporated different versions of the logo, selected a color palette, and chose a font to complement the logo design. The new logo honors and recognizes the donors who make these efforts possible, along with the patients, caregivers, and teams who are fighting cancer. My focus was on crafting a design that remains welcoming and inclusive. I hope to help advocate for a brighter future for both survivors and patients.

Book Cover Redesign for Alabama Moon by Watt Key

Year: 2024
Medium: Adobe Photoshop
Dimension: 1360 x 1030 (Pixel)

Moon Blake lives in the forests of Alabama, away from civilization. The innocent soul steps outside the forest after his parents pass away. Moon gets tangled up with an arrogant constable and his life becomes a full adventure.

I noticed that its old book cover did not convey the feeling of the story well. In this redesign, I signified Moonâ€™s innocence with a pure white background. For the book name, I emphasized the typography and the coloring of the letters; I tried to capture the adventurous theme through the mist surrounding the letters. To create the tension, several letters are imbalanced in different sizes. I created the font in Illustrator by reshaping the letters, resizing the shape, and picking out the color. In Photoshop I designed the mist and reduced the opacity of the brush and pulled down the ink for a scratched look in letters. I hope this design captures the adventurous feeling of the novel.
